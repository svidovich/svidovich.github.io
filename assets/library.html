Narodne Nošnje Jugoslavije / Jugoslavian National Costumes (Download)
by Vladimir Kirin 		This poster book is absolutely gorgeous. The plates are works of art in their own right! You can see the detail in the costumes as you flip through. Costumes are from all regions of the former Yugoslavia. I'm partial to the costumes of Crnagora!
National Costume Paper Dolls! (Download All) A collection of scanned paper dolls for many of the regions of the former Yugoslavia. The original language is Slovenian, but you don't have to know the language to enjoy these! I want to print some out myself, and make little fridge magnets for the kids to play with. Aren't they just adorable?
Narodne Nošnje Severo-Zapadne Bosne / Northwestern Bosnian National Costumes (Download)
by Miroslav V. Draškić
 This dissertation reviews in-depth the costumes common to the towns and villages of north-western Bosnia. The costumes of this region are diverse and beautiful! In some cases, careful drawings outline how one might craft the articles for themself. Detailed renditions of embroidery will help you to re-discover the patterns of your heritage!
Narodne Pjesme Korduna / National Songs of Kordun (Download)
compiled by Stanko Opačić-Ćanica 		Two-hundred and seventy-eight poems from Kordun, from the days of the Graničari border guards, to the battles and struggles of World War II. I have not found another source that contains so many of these hymns!
The Liturgy of John Chrysostom: The SSF Green Book (Download)
compiled by Dragie Cuculjevich 		The compiled works of many composers, but especially of Dragie Cuculjevich who did the compiling ( and some arrangement ), the SSF Green Book is one of the most widely used books of music for the Serbian Orthodox Church in America.
Introduction to the Serbo Croatian Language, Second Edition, 1962 (Download)
By Thomas F. Magner 		A somewhat aged but still very useful introduction to Serbo-Croatian. The author has gone well out of his way to make this a truly Serbo-Croatian manuscript, involving ekavian and ijekavian, as well as vocabulary terms from all over the balkans. Worth a look!
Српски Буквар са Читанком - издање Српске Америчко Канадске Епархије и Српског Народног Савеза /
Srpski Bukvar sa Čitankom - izdanje Srpske Američko Kanadske Eparhije i Srpskog Narodnog Saveza /
Serbian Textbook with Reader - edition of the Serbian American-Canadian Eparchy and Serbian National Alliance, 1943 (Download) 		A dual-purpose learning text for young children. In the first half of the book, you'll learn to read and write cyrillic ( cursive, too! ) while in the second half you'll read brief stories to put your newfound skills to the test. Coming up on 90 years old, this antique workbook is an absolute treasure.
