Yugoslavian Terms Glossary


The language of the Southern Slavs is extremely colorful and full of regionalisms, interesting terms, cultural artefacts, and fun phrases. On this page, we aim to capture some of them!
This page will probably always be "under construction". If you have interesting terms or phrases to submit, please email them to me at samuel.vidovich+yugoslavphrases@gmail.com!

Prelo

Prelos are meetings of villagers during which wool is spun. While the work is on-going, plenty of traditional cultural songs are sung, and plenty of traditional cultural drinks are had!
