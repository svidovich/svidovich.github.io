Hey! Want to do some flashcards? Maybe a quiz?

'BCMS' is the initialism that refers to the language continuum that is Bosnian, Croatian, Montenegrin and Serbian, in alphabetical order. Here you can find fun flashcards to help you learn! You can increase your streak by returning every day and taking a quiz. Try clicking some buttons to get used to the app!
If you speak one of the many fabulous languages of the Balkans and notice that a word in here isn't what you recognize, please feel free to reach out by email! I'd be happy to include regionalisms, dialectics, and slang. Having trouble reading? Try learning to pronounce words first!
